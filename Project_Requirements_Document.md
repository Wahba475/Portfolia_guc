## 2. UI and UX Grading Criteria
> **Note:** UI and UX grading will be subjective (based on the opinion of the assigned Product Manager).

### UX (User Experience)
| # | Criterion | Out of | Comments |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | Information Architecture | 10 | Information layout on pages are divided properly, not a lot of whitepaces, no random buttons, menus are created correctly |
| 2 | Feedback and Responsiveness/ Visibility | 5 | How well does the system tell me what is happening with it |
| 3 | Navigation is Reversible | 5 | DO NOT rely on the back button of the browser!  Also does not have to be a back button on the website itself. |
| 4 | Learnability | 5 | How quickly I can figure out how to use the system |
| 5 | Efficiency | 5 | How quickly I can do a specific task (in terms of number of steps and clicks) |
| 6 | Design for errors | 10 | How can I be prevented from making errors (includes error handling) |
| 7 | Satisfaction | 10 | How I (as a client) personally feel about your system |

**UX Subtotal: 50**

### UI (User Interface)
| # | Criterion | Out of | Comments |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| - | UI elements | 15 | Menus, buttons, tables, etc.. are symmetric and consistent |
